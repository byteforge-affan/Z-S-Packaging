What We Make

Every Box Your Business Needs

From simple shipping cartons to fully branded retail packaging — we manufacture all types of corrugated and printed boxes in-house.

Plain Corrugated Boxes

Strong, cost-effective shipping cartons in any size — built for daily bulk dispatch and warehousing.

Custom Printed Boxes

Full-color branded packaging with your logo, design, and product details — made to elevate your brand on every shelf.

Die-Cut & Custom Shape

Precision die-cut boxes for unique product shapes — mailers, sleeves, inserts, and display-ready cartons.

Mailer & E-commerce Boxes

Self-locking mailer boxes built for online sellers — easy to pack, ship, and unbox with confidence.

Food-Grade Packaging

Safe, durable cartons for food and consumables — meeting hygiene and transport standards.

Industrial & Heavy-Duty

Reinforced multi-layer cartons for heavy goods, machinery parts, and export-grade shipping.
